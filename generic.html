Understanding Modal Music

An excerpt from a 200+ page document to help music students learn guitar, music theory, and read music. The worksheet is intended for intermediate music students who are familiar with music theory and scales, and who are looking to expand their knowledge to include musical modes. The content explains what musical modes are and how they work, offering a step by step guide on how to improvise over modal chord changes. Songs and chord progressions that use modal chords are examined and used as a template to write songs with. Descriptions of each mode's sound and feeling are also examined.

Introduction to the Major Scale

This document is designed for beginner level music students that are looking to learn music fundamentals. The worksheet contains information that explains what the major scale is, how the major scale is formed, and how to apply it to both guitar and piano. It also addresses how to know what the note names in the major scale are as well as a shortcut to be able to know what notes are in each major scale without having to write anything out. This is a section of a 200+ page document to help guitar students understand the guitar, music theory, and a general understanding of music.
